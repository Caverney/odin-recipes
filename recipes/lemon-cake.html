Lemon Cake Recipe

This moist lemon pudding cake has been a family favorite for over 50 years. It makes a delicious lemony sauce on the bottom.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the melted butter and sugar until well combined.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, lemon juice, lemon zest, salt, and baking powder. Mix until smooth.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased 9-inch round cake pan.
  5. Bake for 40-45 minutes or until the top is golden brown and a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
