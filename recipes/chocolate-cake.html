Chocolate Cake Recipe

This recipe uses Jell-O non-instant pudding and cake mix to make an easy and moist dessert. This cake is also good with peanut butter chips.

Ingredients

  • 1 box chocolate cake mix
  • 1 box Jell-O non-instant chocolate pudding
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cake mix and pudding mix.
  3. Add the eggs, water, and oil. Mix until well combined.
  4. If using, fold in the chocolate chips.
  5. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Let cool before serving.
