Banana Cake Recipe

This cake is a very moist banana cake that's easy to make with banana pudding, cake mix, and bananas. It's a family favorite!

Ingredients

  • 1 box of yellow cake mix
  • 1 box of banana pudding mix
  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 cup of water

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cake mix, pudding mix, mashed bananas, eggs, oil, and water.
  3. Mix until well combined and pour into a greased cake pan.
  4. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  5. Let it cool before serving.
